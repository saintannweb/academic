About Saint Ann College
Saint Ann College is an unaided Minority Educational English Medium College (F.No. 87 of 2007 / 22195) established and administered by the Catholic Diocese of Lucknow, a registered society. The college was founded in 1989. It was established with the objective of imparting modern education to Catholic students in a manner that will conserve their religion, language and culture, but admission is not denied to students professing other faiths. The College is named after St. Ann, Mother of Mary. Her maternal care and blessings we seek from Heaven.

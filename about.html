About Saint Ann College

Saint Ann College is an unaided Minority Educational English Medium College (F.No. 87 of 2007 / 22195) established and administered by the Catholic Diocese of Lucknow, a registered society. The college was founded in 1989. It was established with the objective of imparting modern education to Catholic students in a manner that will conserve their religion, language and culture, but admission is not denied to students professing other faiths. The College is named after St. Ann, Mother of Mary. Her maternal care and blessings we seek from Heaven. The motto of the college is "For God and Nation".

The formation of character is the first essential of any sound educational system and therefore great stress is laid on the inculcation of high ideals, gentle behaviour and moral rectitude. Religious instruction is imparted to Catholic pupils only. All other pupils are given a comprehensive course in the value education. In teaching the humanities and science, the aim is to provide sound practical knowledge based on the latest technique of the syllabus of the Council for the Indian Certificate Examination, New Delhi. The college is a co-educational institution where boys and girls grow up together in a healthy atmosphere of equality and mutual respect. This institution has been conducting classes from Nursery to class twelve Twelfth (I.S.C. Examination). It is affiliated to the Council for theIndian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi.

Beside catering for the scholastic needs of the students, College attaches great importance to physical training, Sports and other extra curricular activities.
